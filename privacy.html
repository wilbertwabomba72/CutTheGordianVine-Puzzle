Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

Overview

Cut the Gordian Vine : Puzzle is a single-player game that can be used without an account. The app may display advertising to support the free game experience.

Information We Collect

Cut the Gordian Vine : Puzzle does not request your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or account credentials. Advertising partners may collect device identifiers and related advertising data to deliver ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and support advertising attribution.

Local Game Data

The app stores a small amount of gameplay data locally on your device so the game can function properly. This may include:

  • Unlocked levels
  • Best star results
  • Music on/off preference

This data stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Advertising and Tracking

The app may use third-party advertising services. On iOS and iPadOS 14.5 or later, the app requests permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before allowing tracking that uses the device's advertising identifier or links app activity with data from other companies' apps or websites.

If you deny tracking permission, the app may still show ads, but those ads should not use the device advertising identifier for cross-app tracking.

Children's Privacy

Cut the Gordian Vine : Puzzle is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Deletion

You can remove locally stored game data by deleting Cut the Gordian Vine : Puzzle from your device. If iCloud or device backup is enabled by your operating system, backup behavior is controlled by Apple and your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. Any update will be reflected by changing the effective date at the top of this page.
