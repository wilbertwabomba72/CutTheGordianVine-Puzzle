Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

Overview

Drift Bloom is a single-player offline game. The app is designed to be used without an account, without internet access, and without collecting personal information.

Information We Collect

Drift Bloom does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The game does not request your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or any account credentials.

Local Game Data

The app stores a small amount of gameplay data locally on your device so the game can function properly. This may include:

  • Unlocked levels
  • Best star results
  • Music on/off preference

This data stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Drift Bloom does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, cross-app tracking, or tracking identifiers.

Children's Privacy

Because Drift Bloom does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a future version adds online features, this policy will be updated before those features are released.

Data Deletion

You can remove locally stored game data by deleting Drift Bloom from your device. If iCloud or device backup is enabled by your operating system, backup behavior is controlled by Apple and your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. Any update will be reflected by changing the effective date at the top of this page.
